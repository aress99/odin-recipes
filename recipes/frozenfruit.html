Frozen fruit

My recipe for frozen fruit

This is a great substitute for ice cream. It is healthier and for me it is tastier. Although, people with sensitive teeth might hate it.

Ingredients

  • Frozen berries
  • Frozen strawberries
  • Frozen sour cherry
  • Whip cream

Instructions

  1. Wash the fruit with hot water and put it in a bowl
  2. Add whip cream on top and eat it with a fork.
  3. Enjoy your natural ice cream that is healthy and delicious.

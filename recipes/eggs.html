Scrambled eggs

My recipe for scrambled eggs as a GYMRAT

Who doesn't like eggs ? They are creamy and delicious. As a gymrat I love eggs, because for me they are tasty and protein-rich.

Ingredients:

  • 5x-6x Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • *optional - garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. While the pan is getting hot, beat your eggs well and add the spices.
  2. When the pan is hot, put the butter in it but make sure that it's not hot enough that the butter burns and turn brown
  3. Put your eggs into the pan and stir them for 15-25 seconds
  4. The eggs are done. Put them in a plate and enjoy them
  5. You can grate cheese on them or put a sauce if you want to make them tastier.
